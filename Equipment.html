Equipment For Football

This fun but yet rough sport can require some special equipment so you do not get really injured while playing the sport of football.

  • Shoulder Pads
  • Helmet
  • Mouth Guard
  • Knee Pads Thigh Pads
  • Socks Football Cleats
  • Cup(not required)
